On the quiet night of September 6th, 2025, on CMU's serene campus, the author of this site was suddenly attacked from behind. Whirling around, all he saw was a gray shape flapping away into the night. . .

The genesis of this site was an avian attack on the author (which thankfully left nothing more than a few scratches), from what he can tell by either a Great Horned Owl or a Barred Owl. This prompted a fair amount of Google searching about Pennsylvania birds and owls in particular. If you have had a similar experience, let us know below!
